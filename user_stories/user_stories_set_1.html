The Ultimate Classroom Management Solution

User Stories: "Arwen"

  • As a teacher, I want to easily report and track maintenance issues in my classroom.

  • As a nursing educator, I need to ensure that each classroom is equipped with the specific medical equipment required for my lessons.

  • As a faculty member, I want to suggest improvements for classroom aesthetics through a simple, direct system.

  • As a department scheduler, I need to assign classes to rooms based on their seating capacity and layout.

  • As a young professor, I want to integrate technology into my teaching environment seamlessly.

  • As a user of classroom spaces, I need to quickly access information about the available teaching materials in each room.

  • As a department member, I want to view and contribute to a shared calendar of room bookings and availability.

  • As a professor in a dated building, I need a streamlined process for requesting new furniture and equipment.

  • As a member of a teaching staff, I want to provide real-time feedback on classroom conditions that may affect others later in the day/week.

  • As an educator, I need to easily view the inventory of each classroom before scheduling my classes.

  • As a faculty member, I want a system that allows me to notify maintenance staff about issues directly.

  • As a departmental coordinator, I need to organize classrooms based on specific course requirements like audio-visual aids.

  • As a faculty member, I want to access a dashboard showing the status and upcoming reservations of all classrooms.

  • As a nursing educator, I need a quick way to report broken or missing equipment in the classrooms.

  • As a faculty member, I want to be able to propose and vote on new equipment purchases for classrooms.

  • As a professor, I need to schedule classrooms with specific layouts conducive to interactive learning.

  • As a department member, I want to see a history of maintenance and updates done in each classroom, to track overall department goals.

  • As an educator, I need an efficient way to communicate with the facilities management team regarding classroom needs.

  • As a user of academic spaces, I want to have the ability to leave anonymous feedback about classroom conditions.

  • As a professor, I need to check the availability and condition of special teaching aids like projectors and sound systems in classrooms.

  • As a faculty member, I want a notification system that alerts me when classroom conditions change or issues are resolved.

  • As a department head, I need to oversee and approve classroom configurations for various courses.

  • As a college professor, I want to submit requests for classroom enhancements directly through a mobile or web platform.

  • As a department member, I need to coordinate with other faculty to ensure optimal utilization of shared teaching resources.

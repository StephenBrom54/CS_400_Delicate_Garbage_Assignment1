The Ultimate Classroom Management Solution

Interview 1

Interview date: January 12, 2024

Interviewer: Adam Elias

Needs/ideas:

  • A solution for streamlining teaching in shared rooms.

  • Things would need to manage: actual space, seating # and furniture arrangement, technology and other equipment, like clickers

  • Would love to make suggestions on room looks, condition, etc., without having to go through an unfamiliar facilities request process that only admin assistants might understand

  • Knowing room setup and the stuff inside would help with better classroom scheduling, rather than the current process that only looks at room capacity

Concerns:

  • Wouldn't want it to seem like a black hole or a suggestion box that nobody looks at or addresses, this could easily happen.
  • If it's complicated or even slightly not easy, people won't use it.
  • Be careful that it's not a shadow process of existing facility procedures, so somebody might have to do extra work to feed it into that process.

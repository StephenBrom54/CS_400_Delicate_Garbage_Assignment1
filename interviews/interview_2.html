The Ultimate Classroom Management Solution

Interview 2

Interview date: January 14, 2024

Interviewer: Ben Albright

Needs/ideas:

  • Definitely need tabs on specific students or like a “notes section”

  • Flags for important info, like allergies/medical, guardianship issues/info.

  • If this is going to have something similar to Google Meets or Zoom… definitely put the start and end of the “meeting” at the discretion of the host to start and stop

  • Also, don't let them “chat” with users outside their “network” or classroom.

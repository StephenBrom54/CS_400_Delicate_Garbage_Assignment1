The Ultimate Classroom Management Solution

Interview 3

Interview date: January 15, 2024

Interviewer: Stephen Brom

Needs/ideas:

    Facilities Request Process:

  • Streamlined process for submitting facilities requests.
  • User-friendly menu with commonly used options.
  • Minimize time spent on request submission.
  • Ensuring ease of use for both requesters and administrators.

    Compatibility:

  • Accessibility on both Android and Apple platforms.
  • Consideration for all platforms, including the web, as teachers use personal phones.

    Request Tracking:

  • Real-time tracking of facilities requests.
  • Notifications for pending requests.
  • Direct communication between administrators and teachers regarding requests.

    Year-End Reporting:

  • Comprehensive year-end report with totals.
  • Analysis of classroom usage frequency.
  • Documentation of equipment breakdowns and repairs.

    Time and Material Tracking:

  • Recording time and materials used for repairs.
  • Detailed documentation of resources utilized in fixing issues.
  • Inventory tracking for items used in classrooms.

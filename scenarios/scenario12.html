Scenario 12: "Karrie"

Karrie wants to get feedback from her students about their classroom needs. She uses Classy to create a user-friendly interface that allows students to submit feedback about classroom needs. She can easily view the feedback and identify areas for improvement. She also sets up automated alerts to notify her when new feedback is submitted. This helps her identify areas for improvement and make changes to optimize classroom learning and student success.

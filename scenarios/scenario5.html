Scenario 5: "Haley"

Haley returned to school after the break. Her requests had been completed before the break ended, and employees who were involved in their completion were logged as well.

During her first day back, there were two students absent. She looks on Classy to see if parents have called the school and if those absences are excused.

If the absences are excused, there is a notification on Classy. If it is not excused, Classy will alert Haley at 8:15 a.m., which is five minutes after class is expected to start. The alert includes the names of the students and parents and any contact information.

If Haley decides to do so, she can send an automated text message to the parents through Classy.

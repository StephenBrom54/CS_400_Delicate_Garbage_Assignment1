The Ultimate Classroom Management Solution



Feature: Tracking maintenance requests

Description

As a teacher, I want to easily report and track maintenance issues in my classroom.

As a professor in a dated building, I need a streamlined process for requesting new furniture and equipment.

As a member of a teaching staff, I want to provide real-time feedback on classroom conditions that may affect others later in the day/week.

As a teacher, I want to be able to make classroom requests of varying priority so I can make sure the important things get done first.

Constraints

This will require some aspects of a traditional ticketing system, where someone would need to actively monitor and clear out tickets/alerts/emergencies in order to avoid a cluttered dashboard.

Comments

Based on Arwen's, Haley's, Athena's, and Karrie's scenarios

The Ultimate Classroom Management Solution



Feature: Facility inventory tracking

Description

As a teacher, I want to be able to create multiple inventories for different classrooms so that I can keep track of classroom items.

As a teacher, I want to ensure that each classroom has enough resources to meet the needs of students and teachers.

Constraints

This will also require a ticketing mechanism whereby someone can mark restocks as complete.

This will require teachers to proactively keep inventory updated and for administrators to take care of the reported needs.

Comments

Based on Arwen's, Haley's, Athena's, and Karrie's scenarios
